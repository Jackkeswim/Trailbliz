Sunrise at Maxwell/

August 14th, 2025

Location: state of Perak, Malaysia, 10 kilometres southwest from Taiping

Begin your adventure with an early morning trek up the scenic trails of Maxwell Hill. The journey takes you through lush tropical rainforests, where you’ll be surrounded by the sounds of nature and the crisp, cool mountain air. As you approach the summit, the sky starts to lighten, and soon, you’ll be greeted by a spectacular sunrise.

The panoramic views from the top are breathtaking, with rolling hills and misty landscapes stretching as far as the eye can see. It’s the perfect moment to relax, take photos, and soak in the peaceful ambiance.

Join the sunrise trek at Maxwell Hill and let nature's beauty energize your day!
