Explore Canopy Walk/

March 9th, 2025

Location: Tawau town, Sabah, Malaysia

Tawau Hills Park is famous for its towering dipterocarp trees, including one of the tallest tropical trees in the world. Take a guided tour to marvel at these massive trees, learning about their ecological importance and role in the park’s ecosystem.

For a unique perspective, head to the Canopy Walk, a series of suspended bridges high above the forest floor. From this vantage point, you’ll enjoy a panoramic view of the lush treetops, with chances to spot birds, monkeys, and other wildlife in their natural habitat.

Highlights of the activity:

-Encounter towering dipterocarp trees up close.

-Thrilling canopy walk with incredible views of the rainforest.

-Educational insights about the park’s biodiversity.



What to bring:

-Comfortable clothing, sturdy shoes, and binoculars for wildlife spotting.

-A reusable water bottle and insect repellent.
