Explore the Wonders of Tawau Hills Park

Location: Tawau town, Sabah, Malaysia

Tawau Hills Park offers a perfect mix of adventure and relaxation with its stunning waterfalls and scenic nature trails. Whether you’re seeking a challenging trek or a peaceful picnic by the water, this park has it all.

Discover Breathtaking Waterfalls

The park’s waterfalls, including the famous Tawau Hills Park Waterfall, are a must-see. Surrounded by lush greenery, these cascades provide a refreshing retreat for swimming, photography, and picnicking. The soothing sounds of flowing water create a tranquil atmosphere that’s perfect for unwinding.



Adventure on Nature Trails

For the adventurous, Tawau Hills Park features a variety of hiking trails that wind through its dense rainforest. Each trail offers a unique perspective of the park’s natural beauty, with opportunities to spot wildlife and immerse yourself in the serene surroundings.



Pack your gear and head to Tawau Hills Park for an unforgettable experience of nature’s splendor, where waterfalls and trails promise memories to last a lifetime.
