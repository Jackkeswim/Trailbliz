Maxwell Hill: A Serene Escape

Location: state of Perak, Malaysia, 10 kilometres southwest from Taiping

Maxwell Hill (Bukit Larut) is a serene hill station in Malaysia, offering a peaceful escape with cool weather and beautiful landscapes. It’s the perfect spot for nature lovers and those looking to relax.

Cool and Refreshing Climate

Located at a higher elevation, Maxwell Hill offers a cool climate, with temperatures ranging from 15°C to 25°C.
It's a refreshing break from the tropical heat of Malaysia's lowlands.



Accessible by 4WD or Hiking

Visitors can reach the summit via a 4WD vehicle service, which is the most popular option due to the steep, winding road.
For hiking enthusiasts, there are several trails that lead to the top, offering a closer look at the hill's natural beauty.



Rich Flora and Fauna

Maxwell Hill is home to a wide variety of plants and animals, including rare orchids, ferns, and local wildlife like monkeys. Nature lovers will appreciate the lush rainforest environment.



Maxwell Hill provides a refreshing escape into nature, combining cool weather, beautiful views, and a peaceful atmosphere. Whether you're looking to explore, relax, or enjoy outdoor activities, it’s the perfect getaway.
