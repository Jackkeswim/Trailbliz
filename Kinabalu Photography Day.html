Kinabalu Photography Day/

May 28th, 2025

Location: Mount Kinabalu, Sabah, Borneo, Malaysia

Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of Mount Kinabalu as you embark on a guided photography expedition to capture the first light of dawn from one of Southeast Asia’s highest peaks. Witness the magical hues of sunrise painting the sky, with the rugged granite peaks providing a dramatic backdrop. Whether you’re an amateur with a smartphone or a seasoned photographer with a DSLR, this activity is designed to help you perfect your shots while enjoying the natural splendor of the mountain.

Highlights



Pre-Dawn Hike: Start your journey early to reach the summit in time for the golden hour.

Expert Photography Tips: A professional guide will share tips on framing, exposure, and settings to make your photos stand out.

Scenic Views: Enjoy unobstructed views of Borneo’s lush rainforests, valleys, and nearby islands.

Community Experience: Share the moment with fellow photography enthusiasts, exchanging techniques and ideas.



Equipment Recommendations:



Camera: DSLR, mirrorless, or a high-quality smartphone.

Tripod: Essential for long exposure shots in low light.

Lens: Wide-angle lens for landscapes, and zoom for detailed shots.

Filters: Graduated ND filters for balancing bright skies and dark terrain.

Accessories: Extra batteries, memory cards, and a weather-resistant cover for your gear.



Who Can Join?



Suitable for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and anyone with a passion for photography.

This activity combines the thrill of conquering Mount Kinabalu with the artistry of photography, leaving you with unforgettable memories and stunning visual keepsakes.
