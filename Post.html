Genting Higlands

Location: Mount Ulu Kali in the Titiwangsa Mountains, central Peninsular Malaysia

Genting Highlands is a popular resort destination located in the Titiwangsa Mountains of Malaysia, approximately 35 kilometers from Kuala Lumpur. Known for its cool climate and beautiful mountain scenery, it offers a variety of attractions, including theme parks, casinos, hotels, shopping, and dining options.
