Discover the Perhentian Islands

Location: Approximately 10 nautical miles off the north-eastern coast of West Malaysia in the state of Terengganu

If you're looking for a tropical getaway in Malaysia, the Perhentian Islands are the perfect destination. Located off the northeastern coast, these islands are famous for their pristine white sandy beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and vibrant marine life.

For those who enjoy the outdoors, the islands’ rainforests offer scenic hiking trails with breathtaking views. You might even encounter local wildlife such as monkeys and exotic birds along the way. It’s the perfect escape into nature.

Explore neighboring islands or experience the local culture by interacting with friendly Malay residents. The islands' laid-back atmosphere, coupled with delicious seafood and unique cultural experiences, makes the Perhentian Islands an unforgettable destination.
